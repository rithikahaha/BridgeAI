# BridgeAI — Academic to Industry Skill Gap Analyzer

Every CS graduate has felt it — you finish four years of college and then realise the industry wants skills your curriculum never taught you. BridgeAI was built to close that gap.

Select your university curriculum and target role, and BridgeAI gives you an honest, no-fluff analysis of where you stand, what you already know, what you're missing, and exactly what to do about it in the next 90 days.

## Demo
![BridgeAI Demo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rithikahaha/BridgeAI/main/demo.png)

## What It Does
Compares your academic curriculum against real industry job requirements, identifies critical skill gaps with priority levels (Critical, Important, Recommended), gives you a readiness score out of 100, generates a personalised 90-day action plan to close the gaps, and suggests 3 specific projects to build to become job-ready.

## Supported Curriculums
KTU (Kerala), Anna University, VTU (Karnataka), Mumbai University. Don't see yours? Just paste your subjects manually.

## Supported Roles
Data Analyst, Software Engineer, ML Engineer, SAP Consultant, Business Analyst

## Built With
Python, Groq, LLaMA 3.3 70B, Gradio

## How to Run
1. Open `BridgeAI.ipynb` in [Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com)
2. Get a free API key from [console.groq.com](https://console.groq.com)
3. Paste your Groq API key in the notebook where it says `your_groq_api_key_here`
4. Run all cells
5. Click the Gradio public link that appears to open the app

## Why I Built This
As a CS graduate myself, I experienced this gap firsthand. BridgeAI is my attempt to give every student a clear, honest roadmap from academia to industry.
